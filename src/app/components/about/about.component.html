About Me

I am a photographer whose greatest enjoyment comes from shooting a little bit of everything! My main goal is to photograph genuinely with empathy and respect for subjects. This means I enjoy shooting candid moments and portraits with natural expressions and posing. My photographic style is simple, bright, and narrative. I want to tell the story of an event or person in photos.
